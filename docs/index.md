## My Journey


<table>
  <tr>
    <td style="width:50%; vertical-align:top;">
      <p>
        Mehedi Hasan's journey in public health began in the vibrant yet challenging landscapes of Bangladesh, where he witnessed firsthand the pressing need for sustainable solutions. After earning his Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Public Health & Informatics at Jahangirnagar University, he spent five impactful years at the International Centre for Diarrhoeal Disease Research Bangladesh (icddr,b) working with marginalized communities to address critical WASH challenges.

        Now, as a second-year PhD student & Graduate Research Associate in UofA's Community, Environment, and Policy department, Mehedi is developing innovative health projects and mentoring future leaders, driven by a lifelong commitment to equity and global change.
